About Seattle University

Seattle University, established in 1891, is a Jesuit Catholic university located in the heart of Seattle’s Capitol Hill. It offers a wide range of programs for its 7,500 students across eight schools and colleges.

Known for its strong academic reputation, Seattle University has maintained a top 10 ranking in the Western U.S. for over ten years, highlighting its commitment to quality education and community impact..
